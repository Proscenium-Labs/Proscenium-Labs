## Submitting changes

Please send a [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/7ranceaddic7/{{REPO_NAME}}/issues) with a clear list of what you want done and maybe a little reasoning for the change.  When you send a pull request ([pull requests](http://help.github.com/pull-requests/)), I will love you forever if you include examples of what you propose.

Always write a clear log message for your commits.  One-lines are fine for small changes or tiny edits.  The bigger the change the more that needs to be said about it.  Try something like this:

    
    > An abbreviated summary as the first line.
    > 
    > If your submission is small, that one line will do. The more complicated, in-depth, or expansive 
    > the change is you should be more detailed, but bulleting is fine.  A small 3 - 4 line paragraph 
    > describing the change and its impact would give me warmn fuzzeis.  Levity and Brevity.
